Purpose of Study

In this research, Daniela Mertzen, of the University of Potsdam, Germany, is investigating the processes involved in the comprehension of English sentences. The information collected in this study will be used in the preparation of papers for presentation in international conferences and journals on language. You are eligible to participate in this study because you are a native speaker of English, over 18 years of age.

Procedure

In this study, you will first be asked a few questions about your language background, and then you will be asked to judge English sentences. The entire process should take no longer than 15 minutes.

Compensation

Upon completion, you will be compensated with 4 Euro. During the experiment, you will occasionally be asked to enter a number between 1 and 7. This helps us to exclude data from participants who merely click through the task without paying attention. Please follow these instructions; if you do not enter at least 5 of these numbers correctly, we reserve the right to reject payment. To receive payment, at the end of this session, you will receive an experiment code, and you will be instructed to email this code along with your full name, your address, your BIC and IBAN to our research coordinator. Please note that payment is not instant and it may take several days for the payment to show up in your account.

Risks

There are no known risks involved in this experimental procedure.

Benefits

You will receive no immediate personal benefits from your participation in this research other than monetary compensation.

Confidentiality

Your results will be kept confidential. All data collected in this experiment (including demographic information such as age and gender) will be presented as statistical averages across a group of subjects; your individual data will never be reported in any publication or presentation. To maximize confidentiality, your results will be assigned to a subject number even for our own analyses.

Request for more information

If you have any questions or concerns, do not hesitate to contact the investigator Daniela Mertzen mertzen@uni-potsdam.de.

Refusal or Withdrawal of Participation

Your participation in this research is completely voluntary. You do not have to provide any information that you are not comfortable providing. You may withdraw from this study at any time, though note that doing so means that you agree to forfeit payment.

By participating in this experiment you confirm that

  • You are at least 18 years of age;
  • The research has been explained to you;
  • You freely and voluntarily choose to participate in this research project.
